## Step 3 — Create the multicloud Interconnect (AWS console)

<Steps>

1. Open the [AWS Interconnect console](https://us-east-1.console.aws.amazon.com/directconnect/v2/home?region=ap-southeast-1#/aws-interconnect) — set `region=` in the URL to your `aws_region` (e.g. `terraform output -raw aws_region`).

2. On the **Connection types** page, select **AWS Interconnect – multicloud (paid)**, then **Get started**.

   <Aside type="tip">
     Use **AWS Interconnect – multicloud – Free Tier** instead if you still have quota (one free 500
     Mbps interconnect per AWS Region per CSP). If Free Tier fails with a billing or "free trial"
     error, the quota may be exhausted — paid multicloud is the usual fallback.
   </Aside>

   <Aside type="caution">
     **Cost note:** Paid AWS interconnect is $1.37/hr at Tier 1 / 1 Gbps (APAC local pair). GCP
     transport is separate ($5/hr) — see [Cost
     Estimate](/aws-gcp-interconnect-walkthrough/prerequisites/cost-estimate/).
   </Aside>

3. **Select a provider** — choose **Google Cloud**, then Next.

4. **Select regions** — pick your paired regions, then Next:

   <table>
     <thead>
       <tr>
         <th>Field</th>
         <th>Value</th>
       </tr>
     </thead>
     <tbody>
       <tr>
         <td>AWS Region</td>
         <td>
           Your <code>aws_region</code> (e.g. <code>ap-southeast-1</code>)
         </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
         <td>Google Cloud Region</td>
         <td>
           Your paired <code>gcp_region</code> (e.g. <code>asia-southeast1</code>)
         </td>
       </tr>
     </tbody>
   </table>

5. **Configure options**, then Next:

   <table>
     <thead>
       <tr>
         <th>Field</th>
         <th>Value</th>
       </tr>
     </thead>
     <tbody>
       <tr>
         <td>Description</td>
         <td>
           e.g. <code>demo-interconnect</code> (lowercase, numbers, hyphens; max 100 chars)
         </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
         <td>Bandwidth</td>
         <td>1 Gbps (paid) — or 500 Mbps on Free Tier</td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
         <td>Direct Connect gateway</td>
         <td>
           Select <code>demo-dx-gateway</code> — or paste{" "}
           <code>terraform output -raw aws_dx_gateway_id</code>
         </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
         <td>Google Cloud project ID</td>
         <td>
           Your <code>gcp_project_id</code> (e.g. <code>prj-core-123456</code>)
         </td>
       </tr>
       <tr>
         <td>Tags</td>
         <td>Optional</td>
       </tr>
     </tbody>
   </table>

6. **Review** the summary, then **Finish**.

   <Aside type="note">
     **Free Tier only:** If you see `InterconnectValidationException: Unable to create free trial
     connection`, see [Billing verification
     failed](/aws-gcp-interconnect-walkthrough/troubleshooting/billing-verification/) — or switch to
     paid multicloud (step 2 above).
   </Aside>

7. **Copy the activation key** displayed when the request completes — you need it in Step 4.

</Steps>

The <Tooltip term="interconnect" label="Interconnect" /> status stays **Pending** until the GCP <Tooltip term="transport" label="transport" /> (Step 4) and <Tooltip term="vpc-peering" label="peering" /> (Step 5) complete.
